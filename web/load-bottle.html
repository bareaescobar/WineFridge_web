Description

This premium red wine boasts a velvety texture and a rich flavor profile, highlighted by notes of dark cherry, vanilla, and a subtle hint of oak. Perfectly balanced, it delivers a luxurious, full-bodied experience with a lingering finish that is both smooth and satisfying. Ideal for wine enthusiasts who appreciate depth and elegance in every sip.

Grapes: A sophisticated blend of Cabernet Sauvignon and Merlot, showcasing the harmony of two classic varietals to produce a bold and refined flavor profile.

Flavours: A symphony of dark cherry, vanilla, and oak, complemented by undertones of cocoa and mild spice, creating a complex yet approachable wine.

Food Pairing: Perfectly pairs with slow-cooked lamb, roasted root vegetables, and aged cheddar, making it an excellent choice for both casual and formal dining occasions.

Region: Produced in the renowned Crimson Valley, celebrated for its temperate climate and fertile soils, which contribute to the wine's distinctive richness and complexity.

About the Winery: Crafted by the award-winning Crimson Cellars, a boutique winery dedicated to sustainable practices and the art of small-batch winemaking. Their commitment to quality ensures every bottle captures the essence of the region's unique terroir.
